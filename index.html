Operator background

My background is enterprise IT and public-sector operations, not a research lab. The constant has been owning messy production systems where operator clarity is the whole job.

From 2023 to 2026 I ran the IT and infrastructure function solo for Stewart Holdings Group, a Houston construction holding company with multiple subsidiaries and offices. Identity, security, endpoints, vendors, internal operations: all of it, one person. Before that: endpoint support at David Weekley Homes across a distributed national office footprint, then frontline pandemic-ops support at Harris County Public Health, keeping check-in, registration, and data flows running through surge conditions.

The independent work runs on the same instinct. I build tools the way an operator wants them: useful early, inspectable under pressure, honest about what they don't show you yet.
