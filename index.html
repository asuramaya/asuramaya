Operator background

I come out of enterprise IT and public-sector operations, not a pure research lab path. The through-line has been owning messy real systems where uptime, visibility, and operator clarity matter more than elegant theory.

From 2023 to 2026 I ran the IT and infrastructure function — solo — for Stewart Holdings Group, supporting identity, security, endpoints, vendors, and internal operations across a construction holding company with multiple subsidiaries and offices in Houston. Before that I worked endpoint support at David Weekley Homes across a distributed national office footprint, and frontline pandemic-ops tech support for Harris County Public Health, where the work was keeping check-in, registration, and data flows running during surge conditions.

The independent work is an extension of that same posture. I build tools the way an operator wants them built: useful early, inspectable under pressure, and honest about what they do not show you yet.
